Certified Ethical Hacker

A traning program on Certified Ethical Hacker professional is a specialist typically working in a red team environment, focused on attacking computer systems and gaining access to networks, applications, databases, and other critical data on secured systems. A CEH recognizes attack strategies, the use of creative attack vectors, and mimics the skills and creativity of malicious hackers. Unlike black hat hackers, Certified Ethical Hackers operate with permission from the system owners and take all precautions to ensure the outcomes remain confidential. Bug bounty analysts are expert ethical hackers who use their attack skills to reveal vulnerabilities in the systems.
